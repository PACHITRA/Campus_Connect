## Problem
College clubs use multiple disconnected tools to manage events, registrations, attendance, and certificates.
Students often miss events due to lack of centralized notifications and tracking.

## Solution
CampusConnect is a centralized platform where:
- Students can follow or join college clubs
- Clubs can publish events and notify members
- Attendance is tracked using QR codes
- Certificates are automatically generated after events
- AI helps with recommendations, content generation, and insights

## Target Users
- Students
- Club Admins
- College Administration

## Core Goals
- Simplify event management for clubs
- Increase student participation
- Automate attendance and certification
- Build a secure, scalable, AI-powered system
